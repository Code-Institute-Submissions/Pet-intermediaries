Pug

Pugs a small dog that is very compact and sturdy dog. They got a sense of humor and will try to get your attention and show off. They are stubborn but they love the company of people, they are affectionate and playfull. Center of attention is their place to be. The moles on a Pug's cheeks are called beauty spots. Even for just the 8 kg they can wieght they are great companion.

German Shepard

German Shepherd is a breed from Germany. They are very popular dogs in both as pets and working dogs. They are very intellegient dogs and if you trained them right they can do many things. Very faithful companion that will always be on your side. German Sheperd need much stimulans through doing some work and lot of exercise so you are a very active person the German Sheperd is for you.

Great Dane

Great Dane is a very large breed. This breed is very old. Even for its great size are they very affectionate pets and love to play. They are gentle with children so very good family dog. A loving big dog but they need to be teached some manners when they are young because can be hard to get a control over such a big dog.

Irish Wolfhound

The Irish Wolfhound was originally breed for war and for hunting. But today the Irish Wolfhound is a family companion. On average the Irish Wolfhound is the tallest dog in the world. They still have instict to hunt so be sure for a have fence that is tall. They long walks and be prepared for very much exercise.

Boston Terrier

Boston Terrier have been popular since their creation century ago. Breed to be fighting dogs but today they are gentle, affectionate companion. They get along with anyone in the family and likes any type of home but they need a lot of exercise. So they need a lot of love and exercise. They also very intellegient so you gonna have a great comapanion.

Saint Bernard

Saint Bernard was breed to help find and save lost and injured travelers. Today they are a great family companion and they affectionate with evryone one they meet. Just be warned they drool a lot. They are versatile and excel in the show ring. Even for its size they are indoor dog. They still need a yard they can come out to do some exercise. They are not a exercise buddy so more for cuddle.
