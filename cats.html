Cats a very good companion and don't need to go out. Cats be outside or indoor. For those who don´t want to go out or just don´t need the amount attention a dog gives a cat is more suitable. Some cats can be train if the need seems be needed.

Maine Coon

Maine Coon is the biggest cat breed there is. Even for their size they are called "Gentle Giants" because they are very gentle with their owners. They are the perfect familiy comapnion for they love all in the familiy. They can have a person they love the most. They are very intelligent and can be train if you have the patience and they speak a lot.

Ragdoll

Ragdoll have very unique characters. Also know as "Cat-dog" for their affection to their owners. They work very good as indoor cats. They like to know what their owner does so Ragdolls will follow you where you go so be prepared have not so much alone time. They are also intelligent cats that like challenges.

Holy Birma

Holy Birma is special cat that attach them self to one person. They can get a bit of jealousy if they get no attention. They are very loving cat that is very curious but not so noisy. It is very good to know where your Holy Birma is for because of their curiosity they have been know to be trapped under floors or go on a car ride on the roof.

Siamese

Siamese a very elegeant cat. They are very affectionate with family but that is also a very noisy cat. Siamese loves to talk so can be good be prepared. Siamese cats a very cat so its good to have several active toys so they can exercise so your cat keep healthy.

Bengal

Bengal are very high energy cat and have a very playful side. They need a home that can match their high energy cat. If you are able to do so you gonna have a very affectionate, smart and loving cat by your side in your home. Bengals are also very talkative who is always on alert.

Persian

Persian are know for being quiet and sweet cat. They are not quick on hiss or scratch but they still can get annoyed with loud children or other pets. These cats need to be treated with dignity and gentleness they deserve and if they get that they are very affectionate lap cats that enjoy being around you.
